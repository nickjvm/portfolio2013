Hi. I design, develop, live, eat, and breathe the web. Some call it excessive, I call it life.

My name is Nick VanMeter. By day I work as a Front-End Developer at MINDBODY, a software development company in beautiful San Luis Obispo, California, leveraging the power of technology to improve the health and wellness of the world.

By night, I am a freelance web designer, developer, and user-experience consultant. I have a large set of tools under my belt to build the best in responsive web, including HTML5, CSS3, PHP, jQuery, Drupal, Knockout.js, and Angular.js.

Want to discuss a project? Give me a shout!
